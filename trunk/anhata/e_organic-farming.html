The Organic Farm

In olden days it was India which was growing food in the organic way. Now days, because of the advancement of the technology and influence from the west and also change in mindset of people who are running after fast money has totally changed the organic farming scenario of India. Quite interestingly there are few more places which are still going the organic way which is a good sign. In the ORGANIC FARMING-program every care is taken by us to improve and encourage farmers in the surrounding villages by educating them with Eco friendly and organic cultivation methods which are done by us. In our DEMO-FARMS both sugar cane and vegetables are organically grown, which will be used for the poor who are devoid of nutritious food.
