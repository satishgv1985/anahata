Keep Clean - Make Green

Every morning two couples drive through the village with a garbage can on a three-wheel bike to keep the streets clean. On the three-wheels bike a water tank can be placed to water the trees and bushes.

The plants can be fertilized with own compost. There is a "cleaning day" once per year, when school kids, teachers and the public authority for disposal clean the streets.
