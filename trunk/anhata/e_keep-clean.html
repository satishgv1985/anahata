Keep Clean - Make Green

This is a massive project to retain the past glory of the villages. Due to excessive usage of plastic, industrial waste Mother Earth has succumbed to massive mis-use which is a real threat for the future generations and it has come to a stage where it is a do or die situation for the present generation. In our ECO CLUB-Project we will also visit all the schools in the surrounding areas and educate the children about the urgent need for a clean and green society. This will be a routine thing which will make children think about serving Mother Earth and the need to do so.

Massive awareness campaign will be launched by us to save Mother Earth from this disaster. A team will be working in the villages to clean up the place.

Every village will be given awareness classes on making the surroundings green. We will educate the children in particular, the importance of trees and we will be planting trees in rural areas and also along the road sides.
