Sai Maa Services

Social, Legal, Political and Economic Empowerment for Woman

The women Self Help Groups (SHGs) hold regular weekly meetings, save and repay regularly, and use trained bookkeepers for proper bookkeeping. All SHG members abide by the principles of saying no to child marriages, child labor, domestic violence and wasteful expenditures.

The Organic Farm

In olden days it was India which was growing food in the organic way. Now days, because of the advancement of the technology and influence from the west and also change in mindset of people who are running after fast money has totally changed the organic farming scenario of India.

Keep Clean - Make Green

This is a massive project to retain the past glory of the villages. Due to excessive usage of plastic, industrial waste Mother Earth has succumbed to massive mis-use which is a real threat for the future generations and it has come to a stage where it is a do or die situation for the present generation. In our ECO CLUB-Project we will also visit all the schools in the surrounding areas and educate the children about the urgent need for a clean and green society. This will be a routine thing which will make children think about serving Mother Earth and the need to do so.
